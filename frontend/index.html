The Process
HOW RESQNET
SAVES LIVES
01

Citizen Sends SOS

No login required. Select emergency type, GPS auto-fills location. Done in 10 seconds. Works on any device.

02

Instant Notification

All Admins and Volunteers see the new case immediately via Firestore. SMS sent to the victim's number.

03

Admin Assigns

Admin command center shows case as Pending. One click assigns nearest available volunteer team.

04

NGO Dispatches

NGO dashboard shows resource need by emergency type. Dispatch food, water, medical kits in one click.

05

Volunteer Responds

Accepts → En Route → Completed. Citizen sees real-time progress on their dashboard with a live tracker.

06

Resolved & Logged

Case marked resolved. Full audit trail in Admin panel. Analytics updated. Inventory restocked by NGO.

Our Story
BUILT TO SAVE
LIVES IN INDIA
The Problem
COORDINATION
FAILS IN CHAOS

During India's worst disasters — Kerala Floods, Fani Cyclone, Chamoli — the biggest problem wasn't lack of resources. It was coordination failure. Volunteers didn't know where to go. NGOs didn't know what to send. Citizens didn't know help was coming.

ResQNet was built to solve exactly this — one platform, four roles, real-time Firestore data.
